Whales in Space

A poem

by

Kevin Phan and Zachary Smith

“The diversity of... nature is so great and the treasures hidden in the heavens so rich... the human mind shall never be lacking in fresh nourishment.”


Johannes Kepler

“I feel like I am floating in plasma. I need a teacher or a lover. I need someone to risk being involved with me.”


Francesca Woodman

Won’t you share this space-journey with me? Lights are on the ocean below — blankets of stars wink from above. Come with me, kids, enjoying this journey, these hours, our wonder.

“[She] looked at [her] own Soul with a Telescope. What seemed all irregular, [she] saw & skewed to be beautiful Constellations.”

Coleridge, Notebooks.
